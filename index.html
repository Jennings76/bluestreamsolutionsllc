Privacy & Security

Your meetings stay private. By design.

Blue Mic was built privacy-first. Meetings are shared through secure, expiring codes, not public links that can leak, get indexed, or be accessed by anyone who stumbles on them. Both sender and receiver must be verified Blue Mic subscribers.

Encrypted in AWS

Audio files are encrypted at rest in AWS, with public access blocked and backend access tightly controlled.

Subscriber-only access

Your meetings are only accessible to authenticated Blue Mic subscribers, never exposed on the open web.

Expiring share codes

Share codes expire automatically and are designed to resist brute-force guessing.

No public exposure

No link previews, no browser history, no accidental indexing. Your meeting content stays private.

Verified end-to-end

Every share is verified subscriber-to-subscriber, on both the sender and receiver side.

Secure sessions

Sign-in stays simple, but sessions expire automatically to keep your account safe even on shared devices.

Why this matters. Many transcription tools share meetings through public web links, and the moment one of those URLs ends up somewhere it shouldn't, the meeting is gone:

  • Public links / "anyone with a link" means a leaked URL = leaked meeting content
  • Web viewers get crawled, cached, and screenshotted by link-preview bots
  • Browser history and shared devices can persist content long after the share is "revoked"
  • No authentication requirement means no accountability for who accessed what

Blue Mic was designed to close every one of those gaps.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Does Blue Mic work offline? +
Recording and live transcription run entirely on your iPhone, so you can capture meetings even without a connection. AI summaries are generated when you're back online.
How accurate is the transcription? +
Blue Mic uses Apple's latest on-device speech models tuned for meeting audio. Accuracy holds up well in real-world conditions: speakerphones, conference rooms, and noisy cafés alike.
Where is my audio stored? +
Recordings live on your device by default. You choose if and when anything leaves your phone, and you can delete a recording, and its transcript, at any time.
Which languages are supported? +
Blue Mic supports 35+ languages today, including English (US, UK, AU), Spanish, French, German, and Japanese, with more being added as the underlying speech models expand.
