Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026  ·  Blue Stream Solutions LLC

This Privacy Policy explains how Blue Stream Solutions LLC collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Blue Mic mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information made available through Sign in with Apple, such as your name and email address
  • Audio recordings you create using Blue Mic
  • Meeting titles, transcript edits, speaker labels, summary edits, key points, action items, and related content you create in the app
  • Support requests, feedback, or other communications you send to us

Information from Sign in with Apple

If you sign in using Sign in with Apple, Apple may provide certain account information, such as your name and email address, subject to Apple's policies and your settings. Apple may provide a private relay email address instead of your personal email address. We may store profile data in our own systems to support your account and the Services.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system and version, and app version
  • Basic diagnostic, crash, error, and performance information
  • Timestamps related to account activity and use of the Services
  • Network and request information, such as IP address, where reasonably needed for security and service operation

2. Audio, Transcript & Summary Data

Blue Mic is designed to record meetings and generate transcripts, summaries, key points, action items, speaker labels, and related outputs. When you use these features, we may process:

  • Audio recordings
  • Transcript and summary content
  • Speaker labels and edits
  • Meeting metadata such as title, duration, and timestamps

This processing is necessary to provide the core functionality of Blue Mic. We do not use your recordings, transcripts, or summaries to train AI models.

3. How We Use Information

We may use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Authenticate you and manage your session
  • Provide recording, transcription, summary, editing, playback, and cloud restore features
  • Sync and restore meeting data across devices or app sessions
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Monitor, troubleshoot, and fix technical issues
  • Protect the security and integrity of our Services
  • Respond to support requests or feedback
  • Enforce our Terms of Use and other policies
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

Service Providers

We may share information with vendors and service providers that help us operate Blue Mic, such as providers supporting cloud hosting, infrastructure, authentication, data storage, transcription, and AI-powered processing. These providers may process information on our behalf only as reasonably necessary to provide services to us, and are bound by appropriate data processing agreements.

Legal and Safety Reasons

We may disclose information if we believe doing so is necessary to comply with applicable law, enforce our agreements, detect or prevent fraud or security issues, or protect the rights, property, or safety of Blue Stream Solutions LLC, our users, or others.

Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, asset sale, or similar business transaction, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law.

5. Third-Party Services

Blue Mic may rely on third-party providers to help deliver the Services. Those providers may have their own privacy policies and practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Services, maintain your account, support cloud restore and related app functions, and comply with legal obligations. We typically retain account data for up to 24 months following account closure, subject to legal, security, backup, fraud-prevention, and operational retention needs.

If you delete content or request deletion of your account, we will work to delete or anonymize applicable information within a reasonable period.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including encryption in transit. However, no method of transmission over the Internet and no method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data, object to or restrict certain processing, or withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your information, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

International Users (GDPR): If you are located in the European Economic Area, you may have additional rights including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Our lawful basis for processing is generally contract performance and legitimate interests.

To make a privacy-related request, please contact us using the information below.

9. Age Restrictions

Blue Mic is not intended for anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe that a person under 18 has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

10. International Users

If you use Blue Mic from outside the United States, please note that your information may be processed and stored in the United States or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate. By using the Services, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries that may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. If we make material changes, we may provide additional notice through the app or other appropriate means. Your continued use of Blue Mic after changes become effective constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
